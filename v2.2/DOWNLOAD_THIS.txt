WHAT TO DO AFTER DOWNLOAD:
===========================

1. Extract fps_game_portable_v1.0.zip

2. Read INSTALL_NOTES.txt or START_HERE.md

3. Run the portable build script for your platform:

   Windows:
   --------
   download_sdl2_windows.bat
   build_portable_windows.bat

   Linux/Mac:
   ----------
   chmod +x *.sh
   ./download_sdl2.sh
   ./build_portable.sh

4. Find your game in build-portable/bin/
   - FPSGame.exe + SDL2.dll (Windows)
   - FPSGame + libSDL2.so (Linux)

5. Copy bin/ folder anywhere and play!
